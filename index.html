Momentum is designed with your privacy in mind. All note data you create is stored locally on your device and is not collected or stored by us on any servers. You have full control over your data.

  • Deleting Individual Notes: Within any note list in the app, you can delete a single note by swiping it from right to left.

For data related to advertising identifiers, you can reset your device's advertising ID in your phone's system settings (usually under "Privacy" > "Ads"). If you have any questions about data deletion, please contact us at dreamjournal15@gmail.com.
